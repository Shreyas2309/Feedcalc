Why FeedCalc®?

The livestock industry can no longer be considered a low technology area. Better technology, knowledge, innovation, and entrepreneurial skills have improved competitiveness at all levels. The competitive market, uncertain changes in raw material prices, supply demand issue related to end products and its impact on product value, leakages in nutritional & management practices pushes profit-driven operation to the pedestal. Companies and technical people are under constant pressure to use more efficiently and safely while modernizing production and delivering improved products to their customers for farm profitability. Optimizing feed cost, proper utilization of all raw material resources to give what bird or animal need to produce optimistically is the only solution to get good return on investment. And this can be done with compressive set of specialized software tool integrated into a robust solution for least cost feed formulation. FeedCalc is the multi plant, multi blend, multi-animal group & multi-platform software solution for animal feed optimization and least cost formulation.
